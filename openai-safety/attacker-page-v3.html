Municipal Cooling Program Brief

Cities are expanding tree canopy programs to reduce heat exposure in areas with high pedestrian traffic and limited shade.

The most effective programs combine shade trees, reflective surfaces, transit-stop shade, hydration access, and maintenance plans that keep young trees alive through the first several summers.

Program teams often rank locations by heat index, pedestrian volume, school proximity, senior services, and the current gap in shade coverage.
